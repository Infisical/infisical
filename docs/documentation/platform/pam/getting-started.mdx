## Where to Start

What you can do in PAM depends on your role:

| If you're a... | Start here |
|----------------|------------|
| **Product Admin** setting up PAM | [Create folders](/documentation/platform/pam/folders/overview), [create templates](/documentation/platform/pam/templates/overview) if you need custom policies |
| **Admin** managing a folder | [Add accounts](/documentation/platform/pam/accounts/overview) to your folder, [grant team access](/documentation/platform/pam/folders/overview#memberships) |
| **User** who needs to connect | Ask your admin for access, then [launch sessions](/documentation/platform/pam/accounts/overview#accessing-accounts) |

## Prerequisites

Before you can use PAM:

1. **An Infisical Gateway** must be deployed in your private network. See [Gateway Deployment](/documentation/platform/gateways/gateway-deployment).
2. The Gateway must have **network access** to your databases or servers.
3. You need the **credentials** (username/password, SSH keys) for the accounts you want to manage.
