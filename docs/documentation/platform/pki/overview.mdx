Infisical Certificate Manager is a centralized platform for managing X.509 certificates across your organization. Issue certificates for TLS, mTLS, and device authentication — whether from your own private CAs or external providers like DigiCert and Let's Encrypt.

## How It's Organized

Certificate Manager separates shared infrastructure from team-level operations:

| Level | Entities | Managed by |
|-------|----------|------------|
| **Organization** | Certificate Authorities, Policies, Profiles | Product admins |
| **Application** | Enrollment, Certificates, Alerting, Syncs | Team admins & operators |

This separation means product admins set up PKI once, and teams consume it through Applications without needing to understand the underlying infrastructure.
